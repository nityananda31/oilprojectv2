The philosophy of the Bibleboo

Indonesia has a wealth of life and culture that is reflected in traditional medicine that has been passed down from generation to generation. Herbal concoctions utilize plants that have medicinal properties to maintain health and treat diseases, reflecting local wisdom and the harmonious relationship between humans and nature.

The Balinese Usada Book documents various traditional recipes, including in the *Lontar Taru Pramana, which contains a concoction for babies who cry continuously due to health problems. This concoction consists of dadap leaves, cempaka flowers, onions, and fennel, which are believed to be able to relieve the baby's complaints. The use of this herbal concoction is proof that local culture and knowledge persist and need to be preserved as a natural health solution and cultural heritage.
